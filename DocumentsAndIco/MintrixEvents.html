What are Mintrix Events?

Once a month, a new event comes out. Complete it to unlock massive rewards.



How to unlock them

Once you have come back to the land from Space, inside the village will be an event building. By entering it, you can join the latest event.



How to get the rewards

Every event rewards is purchasable through Mintrix. Though every event is free, the rewards can only be purchased.



Boss fights

Though every event is for free, you can only unlock the boss fights if you have bought and either completed or up to the final stage in Hero Mode.

Overall

The only way to fully unlock each event is by

  1. Have completed the space biome.

  2. Have either purchased the current event or bought the Event pass.

  3. Have completed Hero Mode or up to the last stage.
